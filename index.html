Service Division - Line Item Generator



How many roof areas do you need?

Categories

Step 1

Add, remove, or edit any line item categories as needed. When you're done, click confirm.

Step 2

Determine how many roof areas there will be. Add, remove, or edit any roof area as needed. When you're done, click confirm.

Step 3

Enter the number representing each line item category that is needed for each roof area. Each category number must be separated by a space.

Example: RA #1

If a roof area does not need any repair work done, you can leave it blank.

Step 4

Enter how many locations there are for each repair item. When you're done, click generate.

Step 5

Copy and paste the text into Dataforma or Microsoft Word.
